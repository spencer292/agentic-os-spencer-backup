# Platform Playbook (2025/26)

Per-platform moves that matter. Reuse `mkt-short-form-posting`'s deeper algorithm guides for
TikTok/Shorts/Reels; this is the showing-level summary + the things that change the package.

## LinkedIn (the ICP's home — owner-managers live here)
- **Personal profile, not company page** (≈8x engagement). Post as Roy.
- **Native vertical video, <60s feel, captions on.** Video reach is up ~69% YoY.
- **NO external link in the post body** — it cuts reach ~60%. Put the link in the **first comment**.
- Lead with the **human line**, not the pitch. Authenticity + genuine expertise are now LLM-judged.
- Optimise for **saves and depth of conversation** — end with a real question; reply to every comment fast.
- Length: a short story caption (120-200 words) under the video works; white space, short lines.

## TikTok
- **Completion + watch-time** is king; **saves & shares >> likes**.
- **Keyword-rich on-screen text + caption** — TikTok search is a real discovery surface now.
- 3-5 **niche** hashtags (not #fyp spam). Loop-friendly ending (last frame invites a re-watch).
- "Crazy/authentic" tone is fine; energy > polish.

## Instagram Reels
- Optimise for **sends per reach** ("send this to someone who…"). Saves matter.
- Keyword SEO in the caption (Instagram leans on search too); 3-5 relevant hashtags, not 30.
- First comment: a save/share prompt.

## YouTube Shorts
- **Completion rate** + the **freshness/title** drive it. Keyword title.
- Description: 2-4 lines + tags; a **first comment** with an engagement question.
- Vertical <3 min is allowed and can do well if completion holds — don't pad.

## X / Twitter
- Hook line stands alone (no video autoplay guarantee). Punchy, 1-2 short lines + the clip.
- A thread can extend reach if there's more to say.

## Threads / Facebook
- **Threads:** casual, conversational, like a softer X. Question-led.
- **Facebook:** slightly longer, warmer framing; good for an older owner-manager audience.

## The CTA link (canonical — don't placeholder it)
The primary lead-gen CTA is the **Freedom Score Quiz → https://scoreapp.atpbos.com/freedom**
(hosted on ScoreApp; "The 5-Minute Assessment That Reveals Your Biggest Bottlenecks"). Goes in the
**LinkedIn first comment** (never the post body) and in YouTube descriptions / IG link-in-bio prompts.
Source of truth: `brand_context/assets.md`. Other entry points: podcast, the book, the newsletter,
community. Check `brand_context/` before ever writing "{confirm URL}".

## Cross-platform rules
- **One asset, different wording per platform** — never paste the same caption everywhere.
- **First comment** is a lever everywhere it's supported: the link (LinkedIn), a save/share prompt
  (IG/TikTok), or an engagement question (YouTube). `zernio_post.cjs` supports first comments on
  linkedin/instagram/facebook/youtube.
- Keep the **on-screen hook keyword** consistent with the caption keyword for search.
