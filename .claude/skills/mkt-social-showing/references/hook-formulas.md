# Hook Formulas

A hook = **on-screen text (frame 0) + spoken/first line + visual**, all one message, landing in 3s,
stacking ≥2 triggers. Pick a formula that fits the content's truth — don't force one.

## The formulas (by primary trigger)

| Formula | Shape | Triggers | Example |
|---------|-------|----------|---------|
| **Contrarian Claim** | "Everyone thinks X. They're wrong." | pattern-interrupt + curiosity | "Working harder is killing your business." |
| **Mistake Warning** | "Stop doing X / I almost lost…" | self-relevance + emotion | "I almost got scammed last week." |
| **Open Loop** | "The one thing nobody tells you about X…" | curiosity | "Nobody tells you what success actually costs." |
| **Stakes** | "This nearly cost me X." | emotion + self-relevance | "30 years in security and it still nearly got me." |
| **Question** | "Why do you do this?" / "What if…" | self-relevance + curiosity | "Why do you work 70 hours to live someone else's life?" |
| **List Tease** | "3 things I wish I knew about X." | curiosity + usefulness | "3 signs you're the bottleneck in your own business." |
| **Identity** | "If you're a [them], this is for you." | self-relevance | "If you built a business that now runs you…" |

## The 3-layer build (worked examples)

**Scam-warning short** (Mistake Warning + Stakes):
- On-screen text (frame 0): **"I ALMOST GOT SCAMMED"**
- Spoken first line: *"I almost got scammed last week — and I've spent 30 years in IT security."*
- Visual: Roy to camera, serious. → All three say "even an expert nearly fell for this."

**Spencer racing film** (Identity + Emotion):
- On-screen text: **"LIVING THE LIFE WE LOVE"** (lower third, not on the face)
- Spoken first line: *"Just arrived to see Spencer racing his car. Pretty epic."*
- Visual: the track, the cars. → "this is the life you're working for."
- *Stronger ICP-targeted alt for the caption hook:* **"You're working 70-hour weeks to fund a life you never actually live."**

## Rules

- **4-8 words** on screen, **10-14** spoken. Big text, high contrast, off the face.
- Put the **most surprising/relevant word first** ("Scammed." not "So basically I was…").
- No throat-clearing ("Hey guys", "So today I want to talk about"). Cut to the hook.
- If you can't say *which two triggers* a hook fires, it's not a hook yet.
- For our clips, hand the frame-0 text back to `00-video-studio` so the on-screen hook is burned in.
