# Narration & Scripts

## Pacing

2.5 words per second is natural speaking rate:
- 15s clip: ~37 words
- 30s clip: ~75 words
- 60s clip: ~150 words

Strategic silence enhances -- don't fill every second with speech.

## Tone

- Sound conversational, not corporate
- Use contractions
- Alternate brief and extended sentences
- Read aloud to catch stilted phrasing
- Technical terms only when audience expects them

## Number Pronunciation

TTS reads literally, so spell numbers phonetically:
- "more than one hundred thirty five" not "135+"
- "ninety nine point nine percent" not "99.999%"

## Video Structure

Hook -> Story -> Proof -> CTA

The opening line must intrigue within three seconds: bold claim, provocative question,
contrast, or surprising figure.
