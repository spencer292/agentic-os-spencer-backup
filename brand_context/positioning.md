# Positioning — Got Moles

## Primary Positioning Angle

**The Mole Specialist**

Got Moles is a mole-exclusive control company in Western Washington. While competitors offer moles as one of dozens of pest services, Got Moles does one thing and has done it for 7 years across nearly 5,000 clients. The singular focus is the competitive moat.

> **Do not claim "the only mole-exclusive" company.** Moody Moles (Kirkland), The Mole Man (Puyallup), and Flatline Mole Control also operate in Western Washington. The defensible claim is specialization, depth, scale (5,000+ clients, 219+ reviews), and chemical-free methodology — not exclusivity.

## Positioning Statement

For frustrated homeowners in Western Washington who've tried DIY traps and general pest companies without lasting results, Got Moles is the mole-exclusive specialist that guarantees results through a proven, year-round protection program refined over 15 years — backed by nearly 5,000 clients served and 219+ five-star reviews.

## The Transformation

**Before:** Weekends wasted patching mole holes. Money spent on DIY traps that don't work. General pest companies that show up once and leave the problem unsolved. Embarrassment about the state of the lawn. The demoralising feeling that this will never end.

**After:** The yard is clean. The kids and dog play outside without dodging mounds. No more weekend battles. A specialist handles it year-round. A report after every visit — you always know what's happening. Peace of mind that it's handled permanently.

## Market Sophistication

**Level 3-4: Mechanism/Proof stage.** The Western Washington mole control market has multiple players claiming to remove moles. The customer has likely tried at least one approach. Got Moles wins by showing *how* (specialist focus, proven program, evidence-based approach) and *proving* it (guarantee, 4,973 clients, 219 reviews, ~500 active TMCP subscribers).

## Five Differentiators (Spencer's Own)

1. **100% Mole Focused** — No dilution. Deep, not wide. No ants, spiders, or termites.
2. **Locally Rooted** — Spencer and the team live in the communities they serve. Not a franchise or chain.
3. **Hands-On and Thorough** — Every job starts with physical inspection, evidence-based analysis, custom strategy.
4. **No Guesswork** — Based on mole behavior, movement, and territory. Science, timing, and technique.
5. **Ongoing Control** — The TMCP provides continuous year-round protection, not one-time fixes.

## Unique Assets

- **The Total Mole Control Program (TMCP):** ~500 active subscribers. $100/month. No competitor offers an equivalent ongoing program at this scale. This is genuinely uncontested positioning territory.
- **The Guarantee:** $150 setup fee. If no moles are caught and the problem resolves on its own, the client pays only the setup fee. This is a core brand value, not a marketing tactic.
- **Spencer's Story:** Army veteran (2011-2014). Started in his own yard in Buckley after watching his father battle moles. 7 years, nearly 5,000 clients. This is an E-E-A-T goldmine for SEO/GEO.
