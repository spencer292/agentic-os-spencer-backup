# Voice Samples — Got Moles

Gold-standard sentences that represent Spencer Hill's authentic voice. Use these as calibration when writing in Got Moles' voice.

---

## Sample 1 — The Specialist Statement
> "We do one thing, and we do it better than anyone else: mole extermination. That focus is our superpower. While other companies are spraying bugs, chasing rodents, or offering every service under the sun, we've chosen to specialize."

**Source:** Spencer Hill, Vision Document (March 2026)
**Why it works:** Confident without arrogance. The contrast with generalists is vivid and specific. "Superpower" is playful but earned. Sets the brand position in three sentences.

---

## Sample 2 — The Why Behind the Business
> "Our customers call us when they've already wasted money on DIY traps, had bad experiences with general pest control, or are just completely fed up watching their lawns get destroyed. When they find us, they're not just looking for a service — they're looking for a solution they can count on."

**Source:** Spencer Hill, Vision Document
**Why it works:** Empathy-first. Describes the customer's journey accurately. The shift from "service" to "solution they can count on" elevates the offer without overselling.

---

## Sample 3 — The Mission Reframe
> "You're not just going out to trap a mole. You're restoring peace of mind. You're protecting someone's home. And you're representing a company that stands for doing things right."

**Source:** Spencer Hill, speaking to his team
**Why it works:** Elevates the work from task to purpose. The parallel structure ("You're... You're... You're...") builds momentum. This is how Spencer talks to his people — it reveals what drives the business.

---

## Sample 4 — The TMCP Pitch (Natural)
> "Our Total Mole Control Program is something no one else in the region offers at our level. It's not just a job — it's a relationship. We're there for our customers year-round, giving them peace of mind and protecting the property they take pride in."

**Source:** Spencer Hill, Vision Document
**Why it works:** "Relationship" is the key word — it reframes a subscription as ongoing care. "No one else in the region" is confident but geographically honest. Ends on the customer's pride, not the company's.

---

## Sample 5 — The Guarantee (Values-Led)
> "If we don't catch a mole and the problem resolves on its own — the client only pays the $150 setup fee. No hidden charges. No empty promises."

**Source:** Got Moles program terms
**Why it works:** The guarantee isn't positioned as a marketing gimmick — it's an expression of integrity. The two "No" statements at the end land hard.

---

## Sample 6 — Pain Acknowledgement
> "We've tried everything, but these damn moles keep coming back. I'm tired of patching holes and wasting weekends."

**Source:** ICP persona voice (Jennifer Thompson)
**Why it works:** This is how real customers talk. The frustration is specific ("patching holes", "wasting weekends") not abstract. Use this register when writing pain-led copy — match the customer's actual language.

---

## Sample 7 — Differentiator (Precision)
> "We don't guess. We investigate, confirm, and execute with care. Mole control isn't guesswork — it's science, timing, and technique."

**Source:** Got Moles Core Values — Precision
**Why it works:** "We don't guess" is a brand mantra that appears repeatedly. The three-part rhythm ("science, timing, and technique") is confident and specific. This is the opposite of vague pest control marketing.

---

## Sample 8 — Community Roots
> "We live and work in the communities we serve. Not a franchise or chain. Our technicians grew up here and know this territory."

**Source:** Five Differentiators — Locally Rooted
**Why it works:** Short, declarative, impossible to fake. "Grew up here" is more powerful than "locally owned" — it's personal.

---

## Sample 9 — The Decision Driver
> "I want someone who knows what they're doing — I don't care if it costs a little more, I just want it handled."

**Source:** ICP persona voice (Jennifer Thompson)
**Why it works:** Captures the exact moment the customer is ready to buy. They're not price shopping — they want competence and relief. Write to this mindset on conversion pages.

---

## Sample 10 — Identity Statement (GEO-Ready)
> "Got Moles is a highly specialized, locally trusted mole extermination company dedicated to protecting properties, restoring landscapes, and delivering peace of mind through expert service and year-round care."

**Source:** Got Moles Identity Statement (use verbatim)
**Why it works:** Entity-clear for AI citation. Covers what (mole extermination), where (implied local), how (expert service, year-round care), and why (protecting, restoring, peace of mind) in one sentence.
