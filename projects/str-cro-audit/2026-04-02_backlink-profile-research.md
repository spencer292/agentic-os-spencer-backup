## 4. High-Value Link Building Opportunities (Beyond Directories)

### A. Government & Education Links (.gov / .edu)

These are the highest-authority links available and the hardest to get. Got Moles has realistic paths to several:

| Opportunity | Domain | How to Get It | Difficulty |
|------------|--------|--------------|-----------|
| **WDFW "Hiring a Wildlife Control Operator"** page | wdfw.wa.gov (DA 70+) | Get listed as a licensed WCO. WDFW maintains a public directory at `wdfw.wa.gov/species-habitats/living/nuisance-wildlife/wildlife-control-operators`. Spencer needs WCO certification if not already held. | Medium |
| **WSU Extension resource pages** | extension.wsu.edu (DA 85+) | WSU has mole management publications (FS146E) and Master Gardener programs across King, Snohomish, Pierce, Jefferson, Yakima counties. Offer to be a guest speaker at a Master Gardener workshop. WSU Cowlitz County already runs "Mole Control" workshops. Become a recommended resource. | Medium-Hard |
| **WA Dept of Agriculture license directory** | agr.wa.gov (DA 65+) | Verify Got Moles appears in the pesticide license search at `agr.wa.gov/pestfert/licensinged/search/`. Even though Got Moles doesn't use pesticides, having a clean record in government databases strengthens entity authority. | Easy |
| **WDVA Veteran Business Directory** | dva.wa.gov (DA 50+) | Register as veteran-owned at `dva.wa.gov`. Free. Straightforward. | Easy |
| **VA.gov VetBiz Portal** | va.gov (DA 90+) | Apply for veteran-owned small business verification through `vetbiz.va.gov`. This is a .gov backlink. | Easy-Medium |
