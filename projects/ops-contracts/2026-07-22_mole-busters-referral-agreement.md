# Exclusive Referral Agreement

**Got Moles ↔ Mole Busters**

This Referral Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of **July 22, 2026** (the "Effective Date") by and between:

- **Rainier Power Wash LLC**, a Washington limited liability company doing business as **Got Moles**, with its principal place of business at 718 Griffin Avenue, #905, Enumclaw, Washington 98022 ("Got Moles"), and
- **Mackenzie Parshall**, doing business as / on behalf of **Mole Busters**, with its principal place of business at 7910 142nd Avenue East, Puyallup, Washington ("Referrer").

Got Moles and Referrer are each a "Party" and together the "Parties."

## 1. Purpose

Referrer receives inquiries for mole control and related services that Referrer does not perform. Referrer wishes to refer those inquiries to Got Moles, and Got Moles wishes to pay Referrer a fee for each referral that becomes a paying customer, on the terms below.

## 2. Referral Method — Dedicated Tracking Number

2.1. Got Moles will provision, own, and pay for a dedicated call-tracking phone number (the "Tracking Number") through its call-tracking provider (CallRail).

2.2. Referrer will direct mole-control inquiries to the Tracking Number — by publishing it, forwarding callers to it, or giving it out directly. Calls to the Tracking Number ring through to Got Moles' regular intake line and are recorded and logged like all other Got Moles intake calls.

2.3. The Tracking Number, its call logs, and its recordings are the property of Got Moles. On termination of this Agreement, Referrer will stop publishing or distributing the Tracking Number, and Got Moles may retire it.

## 3. Referral Fee

3.1. **Fee.** Got Moles will pay Referrer **one hundred dollars ($100)** for each Closed Referral.

3.2. **"Closed Referral"** means a new customer who (a) first reached Got Moles through the Tracking Number, or was otherwise identifiably referred by Referrer (e.g., the customer names Mole Busters or Mackenzie at intake), and (b) purchases service from Got Moles — meaning the customer's first invoice is issued and paid — within ninety (90) days of the referral contact.

3.3. **One fee per customer.** The fee is paid once per new customer (per household or service address), regardless of how many calls they make or how many jobs, visits, or renewals follow. Repeat business, upgrades, and recurring-program renewals from a referred customer do not generate additional fees.

3.4. **Existing customers excluded.** No fee is due for a caller who is already an active or past Got Moles customer at the same service address.

3.5. **Attribution record.** The CallRail call log, together with Got Moles' customer records, is the system of record for attribution. Got Moles will apply attribution in good faith, including manual attribution where a new customer credibly identifies Referrer as the source.

## 4. Reporting & Payment

4.1. Got Moles will send Referrer a monthly statement listing the prior calendar month's Closed Referrals (customer first name + city or similar non-sensitive identifier, close date, and fee).

4.2. Payment of all fees shown on the statement is due by the **15th of the month** following the month in which the referral closed, by check or electronic payment as the Parties agree.

4.3. Referrer will complete an IRS Form W-9 before the first payment. Got Moles will issue a Form 1099 where required by law.

## 5. Exclusivity

5.1. During the Term, Referrer will refer all mole-control (and related burrowing-pest trapping) inquiries arising in **Western Washington** exclusively to Got Moles, and will not refer, sell, or direct such inquiries to any other mole-control or trapping provider in Western Washington.

5.2. For this Agreement, "Western Washington" means the counties of Washington State lying west of the Cascade crest.

5.3. This exclusivity binds Referrer only. Got Moles may accept leads and referrals from any source and market its services without restriction.

5.4. Nothing in this Agreement restricts Referrer's own service offerings; it restricts only where Referrer sends mole-control referrals.

## 6. Conduct of the Parties

6.1. Referrer will describe Got Moles' services accurately, will not quote prices on Got Moles' behalf beyond published pricing, and will not make service commitments, guarantees, or claims for Got Moles. Intake, quoting, scheduling, and service are handled solely by Got Moles.

6.2. Referrer may state that it refers mole-control work to Got Moles and may use Got Moles' name for that limited purpose. No other use of either Party's name, logo, or marks is granted.

6.3. Each Party will comply with applicable laws in performing this Agreement, including telemarketing and consumer-protection laws.

## 7. Relationship of the Parties

The Parties are independent contractors. This Agreement does not create an employment, agency, partnership, or joint-venture relationship. Neither Party may bind the other. Referrer is not entitled to any Got Moles employee benefits, and the referral fee is Referrer's sole compensation under this Agreement.

## 8. No Guarantee of Volume

Neither Party guarantees any minimum number of referrals, calls, or Closed Referrals. Got Moles has sole discretion over which referred inquiries it accepts, quotes, and serves.

## 9. Customers

Referred customers who engage Got Moles are customers of Got Moles. All service relationships, records, and revenue belong to Got Moles.

## 10. Term & Termination

10.1. This Agreement begins on the Effective Date and continues until terminated by either Party with **thirty (30) days' written notice** (email suffices).

10.2. Got Moles may terminate immediately on written notice if Referrer breaches Section 5 (Exclusivity) or Section 6 (Conduct).

10.3. **Wind-down.** Referral contacts made through the Tracking Number before the termination effective date remain eligible to become Closed Referrals under Section 3.2's 90-day window, and Got Moles will pay any fees so earned. No fees accrue on contacts made after termination.

## 11. General

11.1. **Governing law & venue.** This Agreement is governed by the laws of the State of Washington. Venue for any dispute lies in the state courts of King County, Washington.

11.2. **Entire agreement.** This Agreement is the entire agreement between the Parties on its subject and supersedes all prior discussions. It may be amended only in a writing signed by both Parties.

11.3. **Assignment.** Neither Party may assign this Agreement without the other's written consent, except to a successor of substantially all of its business.

11.4. **Severability.** If any provision is unenforceable, the remainder stays in effect.
