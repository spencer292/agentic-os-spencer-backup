# H1 / H2 Master Review — Got Moles
**Date:** 2026-05-07
**Scope:** Every page on got-moles.com (139 URLs from production sitemap, excludes /test/ and /lp/)

## Two gates

**Gate 1 — Preserve previous power.**
Pages with historical rank/clicks must keep the keyword pattern they were ranking on. Don't optimize away from a working signal.

**Gate 2 — Duplicate pages use secondary keywords (don't merge).**
Where two pages target overlapping intent, primary page (highest historical equity) keeps the head-term H1; secondary page re-angles to a complementary long-tail keyword + content differentiation.

**Strategic frame:** WA-now, US-later. H1s avoid baking in regional limits unnecessarily. "Mole Control" is the head; "Western Washington" / "[County]" / "[City]" stack underneath.

**Posture A respected:** No body-gripping, scissor, harpoon, spear, or kill mechanism mentions in any proposed copy. "Trapping" is fine.

**Banned claims avoided:** No "WA's #1," no "only mole-exclusive."
