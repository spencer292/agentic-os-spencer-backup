## Executive Summary

Got Moles dominates informational mole biology queries and local city terms across King, Pierce, and Snohomish counties. The gaps fall into six categories:

1. **Cost/pricing keywords** -- high commercial intent, currently unaddressed in blog content
2. **Comparison and "vs" keywords** -- DIY vs professional, method comparisons
3. **Problem-specific keywords** -- damage types, specific locations (garden, foundation, irrigation)
4. **Seasonal/timing keywords** -- spring surge, winter activity, best time to call
5. **Legal/regulatory keywords** -- Washington-specific trapping laws (I-713), what's legal
6. **Geographic expansion** -- Kitsap, Whatcom, Skagit, Island, Mason counties untouched
