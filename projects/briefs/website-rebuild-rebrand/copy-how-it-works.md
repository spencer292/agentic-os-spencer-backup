## [SECTION 2: The 4 Steps]

### Step 1: You Call. We Listen.

Phone us at (253) 750-0211 or fill out the form on our contact page. We'll ask a few questions — where you're seeing damage, how long it's been going on, whether you've tried anything already. No pressure. No upsell. Just a quick conversation so we understand your situation.

We'll schedule an inspection, usually within two business days.

---

### Step 2: We Walk Your Property.

A Got Moles technician comes out and inspects every part of your yard. We're looking for active mole runs, fresh mounds, soil displacement, and entry patterns. This isn't a drive-by — it's a hands-on assessment of what's happening underground.

From there, we build a trapping strategy tailored to your property. Every yard has different mole patterns. We plan for yours specifically.

---

### Step 3: We Set Up and Get to Work.

Professional-grade equipment goes on the most active tunnels. We come back weekly to check, adjust, and respond to changes in activity. If a mole shifts its pattern, we shift with it.

You don't need to be home. You don't need to touch anything. Just leave the equipment where it is and flatten any new mounds between visits — that helps us track fresh activity.

---

### Step 4: You See Exactly What Happened.

After every visit, you get a written report. What we checked. What we found. What we did. If a mole was caught, you'll know. If the yard is clear, you'll know that too.

No vague updates. No "we'll keep an eye on it." You see the evidence, every time.
