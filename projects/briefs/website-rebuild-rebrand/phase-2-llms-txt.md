## What Got Moles Does

Got Moles provides professional mole control services exclusively. The company does not offer general pest control — moles are the only focus. All methods are chemical-free and safe for children and pets.

### Services

1. **One-Time Mole Removal (Eradication Programme):** A focused, one-month intensive removal service for residential properties under 1 acre. Includes initial inspection, equipment setup, 4-5 weekly service visits, and a guarantee — if no moles are caught, the client pays only the $150 setup fee. Total cost: $450.

2. **Total Mole Control Program (TMCP):** A year-round monthly protection plan at $100/month with a 12-month minimum commitment. Includes regular visits, active monitoring, immediate response to new mole activity, and a clear report after every visit. Approximately 500 homeowners are currently enrolled. This is the only program of its kind in Western Washington.

3. **Commercial Mole Control:** Custom-quoted annual contracts for properties over 1 acre, including property managers, HOAs, sports facilities, schools, and commercial estates.

## About Spencer Hill

Spencer Hill is the founder and owner of Got Moles. He served in the US Army as an infantryman from 2011 to 2014. Spencer was born and raised in Buckley, Washington, and now lives in Enumclaw. He founded Got Moles in 2017 after years of trapping moles in his own yard and for neighbours in the community.
