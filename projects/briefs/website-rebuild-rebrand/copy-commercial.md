## [SECTION 6: FAQ]

**How is commercial pricing structured?**
Every commercial contract is quoted individually after a site inspection. Scope, acreage, and complexity determine the price. We don't apply residential pricing to commercial sites.

**Do you cover multiple properties under one contract?**
Yes. Several of our property management clients have us covering multiple sites under one account, with consolidated reporting and consistent service across all of them.

**What documentation do you provide?**
A written report after every visit. Activity found, actions taken, equipment status, and recommendations. This is standard — not an optional extra.

**Are your methods safe for public spaces and schools?**
Yes. All Got Moles methods are chemical-free. No poisons or toxicants are used on any site. Safe for public spaces, schools, and anywhere children and pets are present.

**How quickly can you respond to new activity?**
Within the terms of your contract. Most commercial clients are on weekly or fortnightly visit schedules, with priority response for reported new activity between visits.
