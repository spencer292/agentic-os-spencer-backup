## Content Standards for All Blog Posts

Every post must follow these rules for GEO readiness:

1. **BLUF opening** — first 2-3 sentences are a standalone, quotable answer
2. **FAQ schema** — at least 2-3 question/answer pairs per post
3. **Fact density** — a stat or data point every 150-200 words
4. **Expert attribution** — "Spencer Hill, founder of Got Moles and mole control specialist since 2017"
5. **Source citations** — link to WSU, WDFW, Seattle Public Utilities, or other authoritative sources
6. **Internal links** — link to at least 2 service pages and 1-2 other blog posts
7. **CTA** — every post ends with a clear call to action (contact, TMCP, or city page)
8. **Last updated date** — displayed on the page and in schema
9. **Humanizer gate** — all copy through tool-humanizer before publishing
10. **Word count** — minimum 1,000 words for new posts; aim for 1,200-1,500 on priority topics
