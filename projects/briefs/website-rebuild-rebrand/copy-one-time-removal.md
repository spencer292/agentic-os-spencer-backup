## [SECTION 4: What We Need from You]

### H2: Your Part

We handle the trapping. Here's what helps us get the best results:

**Don't touch the equipment.** Every piece is placed deliberately. Moving it — even a little — throws off the setup and can leave scent that moles avoid. Any equipment damaged or lost from handling is the customer's responsibility.

**Flatten mounds between visits.** After each visit, lightly rake and flatten any mole mounds. This isn't busywork — it's how we tell fresh activity from old damage when we come back.

**Let us handle disposal.** We place caught moles in a pet waste bag and dispose of them in your bin or bury them on-site. If you have a preferred method, let us know in advance.
