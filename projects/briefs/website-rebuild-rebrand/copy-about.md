## [SECTION 2: Spencer's Origin Story]

### H2: How It Started

> ⚠️ **[PENDING SPENCER'S WRITTEN CONFIRMATION — verbally approved, adjustments expected]**

For Spencer Hill, it started with his father.

Spencer's dad took real pride in his garden. It wasn't just a lawn — it was something he'd built and maintained over years. Then the moles came. And they didn't just damage the garden. They wrecked the peace of the whole household.

Spencer watched his father spend weekend after weekend fighting it. They tried everything — store-bought traps, home remedies, products that promised results. Every time they thought they'd cracked it, the moles came back.

That stuck with him. Years later, when Spencer started trapping moles himself — first in his own yard in Buckley, then for neighbors, then for the wider community — he understood something most pest control companies miss. This isn't just a lawn problem. It's a stress problem. The people calling aren't just frustrated. They're exhausted from a fight they can't win on their own.

## [SECTION 3: Building the Company]

### H2: From Buckley to 70+ Communities

In 2017, Spencer made it official and founded Got Moles. The business grew through word of mouth first — because he solved the problem properly, and people told their neighbors.

Today, Got Moles has served nearly 5,000 clients across Western Washington, covering more than 70 communities across King, Pierce, Thurston, and Snohomish Counties.

## [SECTION 4: Why Moles Only]

### H2: Why We Only Do Moles

Most pest companies offer everything. Ants. Spiders. Termites. Rodents. And somewhere on the list, moles.

Spencer made a different choice. He'd seen what generalists delivered for mole problems — a technician who'd done a spider job that morning, using whatever was on the truck, following a generic protocol. Inconsistent results. Constant callbacks.

Got Moles exists because mole control deserves the same focus any serious trade gives its craft. We went deep instead of wide. That focus is why our results are different.

## [SECTION 5: The Veteran]

### H2: Built on Discipline

Spencer served in the US Army as an infantryman from 2011 to 2014. He doesn't bring it up for marketing points — but the discipline and the refusal to cut corners carried straight into how Got Moles operates.

Show up when you say you will. Do the job properly. Report the results honestly. Spencer didn't learn those from a business book. He lived them before Got Moles existed.
