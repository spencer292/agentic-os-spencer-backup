## [SECTION 3: How the TMCP Works]

### H2: How It Works

Your Got Moles technician visits on a regular schedule — weekly during active periods, monthly during maintenance. Every visit follows the same process:

**Monitor.** We check the entire property for signs of new mole activity. Fresh mounds, tunnel runs, soil displacement — we know what to look for and we catch problems before they take hold.

**Respond.** When moles are detected, we act the same day. Equipment goes in, strategies get adjusted, activity gets tracked. No waiting for a call-back or scheduling a separate appointment. It's already covered.

**Report.** After every visit, you get a clear written summary. What we checked, what we found, what we did. You're never left wondering what's happening on your property.

## [SECTION 8: FAQ]

### H2: Common Questions About the TMCP

**How often do you visit?**
Weekly during active mole season, shifting to monthly once we've established full control. If new activity pops up at any point, we increase visit frequency at no extra cost.

**Is there a contract?**
The TMCP runs on a 12-month minimum commitment, billed monthly. We ask for 30 days' notice if you want to cancel after the initial term.

**What if I've never used Got Moles before?**
Most TMCP clients start with our one-time removal program. Once the active infestation is cleared, we move them to ongoing protection. You can also jump straight to the TMCP if you'd prefer.

**What does the report include?**
Each report covers: areas inspected, mole activity found (or confirmed clear), actions taken, equipment status, and any recommendations. You'll always know what happened.

**Is the TMCP available for commercial properties?**
Commercial properties are serviced under separate annual contracts with custom pricing. Get in touch for a commercial quote.

**What if I sell my home?**
The TMCP can transfer to the new homeowner, or you can cancel with 30 days' notice.

**Can I cancel early?**
An early cancellation fee applies within the first 12 months. After that, cancel any time with 30 days' notice.
