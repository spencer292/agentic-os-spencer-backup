1. **"How do I know if I have moles in my [city] yard?"**
   → Signs: raised ridges, volcano-shaped mounds, soft/spongy spots in lawn.

2. **"How much does mole removal cost in [city]?"**
   → Got Moles: $450 flat rate (residential under 1 acre). Includes 4-5 weekly visits + guarantee.

3. **"Are moles common in [city], WA?"**
   → Localized answer based on soil type, proximity to green corridors, typical lot sizes.

4. **"What kind of moles live in [city]?"**
   → Townsend's mole (most common), Pacific Coast mole, occasionally Shrew mole near wooded areas.

5. **"Is mole trapping safe for my pets?"**
   → Yes. Got Moles uses chemical-free methods only. All equipment placed underground.

6. **"How long does mole removal take in [city]?"**
   → Typically 3-5 weekly visits (about one month) for one-time removal.

7. **"Do you guarantee your mole removal service?"**
   → Yes. $150 setup fee collected upfront. If no moles caught, you pay only the $150.
