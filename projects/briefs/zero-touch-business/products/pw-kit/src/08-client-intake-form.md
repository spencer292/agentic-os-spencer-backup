# Pressure Washing Business Starter Kit
**File 08 — Client Intake Forms (Residential + Commercial)** | v1.0 | 2026-07-23

> **How to use this template:** Two forms below — one for homeowners, one for businesses. Copy each into its own Google Doc or Google Form. Fill the {PLACEHOLDERS} in the header with your business info. Every question here earns its place: it either prices the job, prevents a surprise on service day (no water, locked gate, loose dog), or tells you where your leads come from. Don't add questions you won't use.
