## How to use this

Copy the template below into your invoicing tool, a Google Doc, or a Word file. Replace every `{PLACEHOLDER}` with your info. Send it the same day you finish the job — invoices sent within 24 hours get paid roughly twice as fast as invoices sent "when you get around to it." State the due date plainly, and enforce your late fee once. You'll rarely have to enforce it twice.
