## What to Expect — Please Read

- **Stain limits:** rust, oil, paint, irrigation (mineral), tannin, and deep organic stains **may not fully lift**. We treat them and manage expectations honestly; where we don't expect full removal, we'll say so before we start.
- **Older surfaces:** cleaning can reveal or highlight pre-existing conditions (chalky/faded siding, failing paint, wood fuzzing, cracked concrete, worn seals). We document these before we begin and are not responsible for conditions that existed before service.
- **Prep:** please close windows/doors, move vehicles and loose items off the work area, secure pets indoors, and have the outdoor spigot on. Full prep list is in your welcome packet.
- **Weather:** exterior work is weather-dependent. If rain, wind, or freezing temps force a change, we'll reschedule at no penalty.
