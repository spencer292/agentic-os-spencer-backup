## Description

You can figure out how to clean a house. What sinks new cleaning businesses is everything around the cleaning: quoting too low, working without a contract, chasing invoices, and driving 40 minutes between $100 jobs.

This kit is the paperwork layer, done:

**Price it right**
- Pricing Calculator (Google Sheets) — type in square footage, cleaning type, frequency, add-ons; get a flat-rate quote with a built-in minimum-job floor so you physically can't quote a money-losing job
- Job Costing Sheet — your true hourly earnings including drive time, per client, ranked
- Income & Expense Tracker — monthly P&L plus common tax-deduction categories

**Protect the work**
- Residential Service Agreement + Commercial Cleaning Contract — scope, access, breakage policy with liability caps, cancellation and lockout fees, re-clean guarantee
- Cancellation & No-Show Policy insert, Liability-aware Quote/Estimate and Invoice templates

**Run the route**
- Recurring Client & Route Tracker — day-of-week route grid and revenue per route day
- Client Intake Forms (residential + commercial), Client Welcome Packet, Price-Increase Letter, Complaint-Response email templates
- Crew-ready Standard / Deep / Move-Out cleaning checklists
- Zero-to-First-Client startup checklist

**Format:** Google Sheets and Google Docs copy-links (edit your own copies forever) + printable PDFs. Instant delivery.

**Who it's for:** solo operators and small crews starting or tightening up a residential/commercial cleaning business in the US.

**Who it's not for:** franchises with corporate paperwork, and anyone looking for legal advice — these templates are starting points; have a local attorney review contracts before use.
