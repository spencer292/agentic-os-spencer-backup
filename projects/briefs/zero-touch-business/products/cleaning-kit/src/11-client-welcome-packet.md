- **What we bring:** All supplies and equipment. If you'd prefer we use a specific product you provide (for floors, stone counters, or anything else), just leave it out and tell us once — we'll note it on your file permanently.
- **How long it takes:** Your regular clean runs about {ESTIMATED DURATION}. The first clean usually runs longer because we're bringing the home up to our baseline.

## Rescheduling & Cancellations (Short Version)

Life happens — we get it. Here's the deal we make with every client:

- Give us at least **{24 / 48} hours' notice** to reschedule or skip a visit, and there's no charge. Ever.
- Cancel inside that window, or if we arrive and can't get in, a **{$XX / X%} fee** applies. That fee exists because your time slot was reserved for you and our team gets paid either way.
- Need to change your regular day or frequency? Just ask — we'll find the next best slot.

The full policy is in your service agreement. The short version is: talk to us early and we'll almost always find a way to make it work.

## Pets

We love them. Truly — some of our favorite clients have four legs.

- Tell us your pets' names and personalities so the team knows who's greeting them.
- If your pet is anxious around vacuums or strangers, please crate them or set them up in a closed room on cleaning day. It's safer and less stressful for everyone.
- We'll always close exterior doors and gates behind us, and we'll never let a pet out — but please tell us about any escape artists so we can be extra careful.
- We're happy to clean around pets; we just can't clean up significant pet accidents as part of a standard visit. If it happens, tell us and we'll quote the extra time fairly.

Here's our promise, in plain English: **if we missed something, tell us within {24 / 48} hours and we'll come back and make it right — free.** No arguing, no interrogation, no hurt feelings.

How to flag an issue:

1. Text or email us a quick description (photos help a lot) within {24/48} hours of your clean.
2. We'll respond within {RESPONSE TIME} and schedule a return visit — usually within {X business days}.
3. We log every miss against our checklist so it doesn't happen again.

We would much rather hear about a problem than lose you quietly. The clients who tell us when we miss are the ones we keep for years.

## How to Help Us Serve You Best

A few small things that make a big difference:

- **Tell us your priorities.** If the kitchen matters more to you than the guest room, say so — we'll weight our time accordingly.
- **Point out the quirks.** The stubborn shower door, the antique table that hates water, the drawer that sticks — the more we know, the better we protect your home.
- **Give feedback early and often.** "The floors were great, but the mirrors had streaks" is gold to us. We can only fix what we know about.
- **Keep us posted on changes.** New pet, renovation, new alarm code, houseguests — a quick text keeps everything running smooth.
