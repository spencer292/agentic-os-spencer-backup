# RESIDENTIAL CLEANING SERVICE AGREEMENT

## 1. Parties

This Residential Cleaning Service Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of **{DATE}** by and between:

**Service Provider:** {BUSINESS NAME}, a {ENTITY TYPE — e.g., sole proprietorship / LLC} located at {BUSINESS ADDRESS}, phone {BUSINESS PHONE}, email {BUSINESS EMAIL} ("Company").

**Client:** {CLIENT NAME}, residing at {SERVICE ADDRESS}, phone {CLIENT PHONE}, email {CLIENT EMAIL} ("Client").

The property to be cleaned is located at: **{SERVICE ADDRESS}** (the "Property").

## 2. Scope of Services

Company will provide **{SERVICE TYPE — e.g., recurring standard cleaning / deep cleaning / move-out cleaning}** at the Property on the schedule described in Section 3.

**Included in each standard cleaning visit** (check all that apply):

- [ ] Dust all accessible surfaces (furniture, shelves, sills, baseboards within reach)
- [ ] Vacuum all carpets, rugs, and floors
- [ ] Mop hard-surface floors
- [ ] Kitchen: wipe countertops, exterior of appliances, sink, stovetop, cabinet fronts
- [ ] Kitchen: clean microwave interior
- [ ] Bathrooms: clean and disinfect toilets, tubs, showers, sinks, counters, mirrors
- [ ] Make beds / change linens (linens provided by Client)
- [ ] Empty trash and replace liners
- [ ] Wipe light switches, door handles, and high-touch points
- [ ] Tidy and straighten common areas
- [ ] Other: {CUSTOM TASK}
- [ ] Other: {CUSTOM TASK}

**Not included unless quoted separately as an add-on:** interior windows, inside oven, inside refrigerator, laundry, dishes beyond loading the dishwasher, walls, ceilings, blinds (beyond dusting), garages, patios/decks, organizing/decluttering, post-construction debris, biohazards (mold, pest waste, bodily fluids), lifting or moving items over {WEIGHT — e.g., 25} lbs, and cleaning at heights requiring more than a 2-step stool.

Add-on services and pricing are listed in **Exhibit A** (attach your quote or price list).

**Frequency:** {WEEKLY / BI-WEEKLY / EVERY 4 WEEKS / ONE-TIME}, on {DAY(S)}, with an arrival window of {ARRIVAL WINDOW — e.g., 9:00 AM – 11:00 AM}. Exact arrival within the window may vary with route and traffic.

**Access method** (check one):

## 4. Pricing & Payment

- **Rate per visit:** ${RATE} for standard recurring service as scoped above.
- **First visit / deep clean:** ${INITIAL RATE} (first visits take longer; this is normal).
- **Add-ons:** billed per Exhibit A when requested.
- **Payment is due {AT TIME OF SERVICE / WITHIN X DAYS OF INVOICE}** by {PAYMENT METHODS — e.g., card on file, ACH, check}.
- Late payments over {X} days may incur a late fee of {LATE FEE — e.g., $25 or 1.5% per month, whichever is greater, where permitted by law}.
- Company may adjust rates with at least **{30} days' written notice**. Continued service after the notice period constitutes acceptance of the new rate.
- If the condition of the Property is materially different from what was quoted (e.g., significantly heavier soil, post-party condition), Company will notify Client before proceeding with any price adjustment.

## 5. Supplies & Equipment

- [ ] **Company provides** all cleaning supplies and equipment (default).
- [ ] **Client provides** the following: {LIST — e.g., preferred products, vacuum}.

If Client requests specific products (e.g., fragrance-free, specific brands), Client will supply them or Company will source them at cost plus handling. Company is not responsible for results or damage caused by Client-supplied products used at Client's direction.

## 6. Pets

Client has the following pets at the Property: {PETS / NONE}.

- Client agrees to secure any pet that may be anxious, aggressive, or a flight risk during service.
- Cleaners will not clean up pet accidents involving feces, urine, or vomit beyond incidental spot-cleaning; heavy pet waste cleanup is out of scope.
- Company is not responsible for pets that escape through doors left accessible per Client's access instructions, provided staff followed the agreed entry/exit procedure.

## 7. Breakage, Damage & Liability

- Company will treat the Property and its contents with care. If Company damages or breaks an item, Client must report it within **{48} hours** of the visit. Company will, at its option, repair, replace with an item of like kind and value, or credit the item's reasonable value.
- Client agrees to secure or notify Company in writing of items that are **irreplaceable, fragile, or of unusual value** (heirlooms, collectibles, items over ${VALUE THRESHOLD — e.g., 250}). Company's responsibility for any single item not disclosed in writing is capped at **${PER-ITEM CAP — e.g., 250}**.
- Except where prohibited by law, Company's total aggregate liability under this Agreement is limited to the **greater of the amount paid by Client in the {3} months preceding the claim or ${LIABILITY CAP}**. Neither party is liable for indirect, incidental, or consequential damages.
- Company carries general liability insurance: carrier {INSURER}, policy limits {LIMITS}. Certificate available on request.
- Pre-existing damage, wear, or defects (loose fixtures, scratched surfaces, unstable shelving) are not Company's responsibility; staff will flag anything they notice.

## 8. Satisfaction & Re-Clean Policy

Quality matters more than excuses. If Client is not satisfied with any area cleaned during a visit:

1. Client notifies Company within **{24} hours** of the visit (photos help).
2. Company will return within **{2–3} business days** to re-clean the specific areas at **no charge**.
3. Refunds are not issued in lieu of a re-clean, but Company may offer a credit at its discretion.

Complaints raised after the notice window, or after the area has been used/re-soiled, are handled case-by-case.

## 9. Cancellations, Lockouts & Skipped Visits

- **Cancellation/reschedule notice:** at least **{48} hours** before the scheduled visit — no charge.
- **Late cancellation** (less than {48} hours): fee of **${LATE CANCEL FEE / X% of visit rate}**.
- **Lockout:** if cleaners arrive and cannot access the Property (no key, changed code, aggressive pet, occupant refuses entry), a lockout fee of **${LOCKOUT FEE / X% of visit rate}** applies.
- Skipping visits increases the work at the next visit; after {2} consecutive skips, Company may re-quote the next visit as a deeper clean.
- Company will give as much notice as possible if it must reschedule (illness, weather, emergency) and will not charge for Company-initiated changes.

## 10. Safe Working Conditions

Client agrees to provide a safe working environment: functioning utilities (water, power, reasonable temperature), no unsafe structural conditions, and no illegal activity on site during service. Company may pause or end a visit if staff safety is at risk, with the visit billed pro-rata for work completed.

## 11. Non-Solicitation of Staff

Company invests heavily in hiring and training. During this Agreement and for **{12} months** after it ends, Client agrees not to directly hire or contract Company's current or former staff for cleaning services without Company's written consent. If Client does, Client agrees to pay a placement fee of **${PLACEMENT FEE — e.g., 2,500}** as reasonable compensation for recruiting and training costs.

## 12. Photos (Optional Permission)

Company may take before/after photos of work areas for quality control. **Marketing use is separate and optional:**

- [ ] **Yes** — Company may use before/after photos of cleaned areas in marketing (no address, no identifying details, no people or personal items in frame).
- [ ] **No** — photos for internal quality control only.

## 13. Term & Termination

This Agreement begins on {START DATE} and continues until terminated. **Either party may terminate at any time with {14 / 30} days' written notice** (email counts). Client remains responsible for payment for all services performed. On termination, Company returns all keys and deletes stored access codes.

Company may terminate immediately for non-payment, unsafe conditions, or abusive treatment of staff.

## 14. General Terms

- **Independent contractor:** Company is an independent contractor, not Client's employee.
- **Entire agreement:** This Agreement plus Exhibit A is the entire agreement and replaces prior discussions. Changes must be in writing (email confirmation is acceptable).
- **Severability:** If any provision is unenforceable, the rest remains in effect.
- **Governing law:** This Agreement is governed by the laws of the State of {STATE}.
