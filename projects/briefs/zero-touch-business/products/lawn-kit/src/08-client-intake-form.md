# Lawn Care Business Starter Kit
**File 08 — Client Intake Forms (Residential + Commercial)** | v1.0 | 2026-07-23

> **How to use this template:** Two forms below — one for homeowners, one for commercial/HOA. Copy each into its own Google Doc or Google Form. Fill the {PLACEHOLDERS} in the header with your business info. Every question earns its place: it either prices the job, prevents a surprise on the first cut, or tells you where your leads come from. Capture lot size and gate/obstacle info on every residential lead — those two things are what turn a phone number into an accurate quote.
