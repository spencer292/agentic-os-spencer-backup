## How to use this

Copy the template below into your invoicing tool, a Google Doc, or a Word file. Replace every `{PLACEHOLDER}` with your info. Send it the same day you finish the job — or, on flat-monthly accounts, on the same day every month (the 1st is clean and easy to remember). Invoices sent promptly get paid roughly twice as fast as invoices sent "when you get around to it." Set your due date, state it plainly, and enforce your late fee once. You'll rarely have to enforce it twice.

Two versions below: **(A)** per-visit, for per-cut clients, and **(B)** flat-monthly, for season-contract clients. Use whichever matches the account.
