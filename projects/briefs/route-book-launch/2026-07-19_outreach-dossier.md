# The Route — Outreach Dossier

**Purpose:** everything needed to mail 20 signed copies with handwritten notes — who, where, why them, and what to reference in each note. Researched 2026-07-19; all addresses are publicly published (company sites, org contact pages, registries). No guessed addresses — targets without a public address are marked **ask-first**.

**The book:** *The Route: Stop Renting Your Customers and Start Owning Them* by Spencer Hill
- **Amazon:** https://www.amazon.com/dp/B0H6ZX85DK · ASIN `B0H6ZX85DK` · ISBN-13 `979-8180586025`
- Paperback, 120 pages, $16.31 · published June 28, 2026 · 5.0★ (2 ratings as of 07-19)
- **Core thesis:** stop renting customers (leads, one-time jobs) and start owning them — territory, membership, recurring revenue. Aimed at ALL service trades: pest, landscaping, cleaning, HVAC, plumbing, and more.
- **Positioning note for outreach:** the book itself is a recurring-revenue/service-business book, not a memoir — so lead every pitch with the business idea ("one-truck operation → recurring-revenue machine"), and use the Army-veteran + mole-niche story as the credibility/color. For the veteran lane, flip it: vet-to-vet note first, book thesis second.

> Include an insert card in each package: cover, the Amazon link above (or a short link), and spencer@got-moles.com.
