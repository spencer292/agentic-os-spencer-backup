# The Route — Book Launch Outreach

## Goal
Turn 20 author copies of Spencer's book *The Route* into momentum: reviews, podcast invites, mentions, and word-of-mouth sales beyond the first 19 friends-and-family buyers.

## The book
- Title: *The Route: Stop Renting Your Customers and Start Owning Them* by Spencer Hill (side project — not under the Got Moles brand)
- Amazon: https://www.amazon.com/dp/B0H6ZX85DK (ASIN B0H6ZX85DK, ISBN-13 979-8180586025) — paperback, 120 pp, $16.31, pub. June 28, 2026
- Thesis: recurring-revenue/membership model for service businesses (all trades), told through building a one-truck operation into a recurring-revenue machine; Army-veteran founder story as the color
- Inventory: 20 physical copies on hand for outreach

## Strategy
Handwritten note + signed copy mailed to people with an audience that overlaps the book's readers, across four lanes:
1. **Home-service / contractor world** — podcast hosts and operators (Tommy Mello, Jobber's Masters of Home Service, etc.)
2. **Veteran entrepreneurs** — authors, podcasters, and vet-business orgs (veteran-to-veteran note angle)
3. **Pest-control trade** — industry podcasts, magazines, associations (a mole-only founder is a novel story here)
4. **Local Washington** — local media and WA-based creators (hometown founder angle)

## Deliverables
- `2026-07-19_outreach-dossier.md` — full contact dossier: mailing addresses, emails, guest-pitch routes, personalization hooks, per-target fit notes
- Send tracker (columns in the dossier): tier, sent date, response, follow-up

## Acceptance criteria
- Every target has either a verified public mailing address or an explicit "no public address + best alternative route" — no guessed addresses
- Each target has a personalization hook so no note is generic
- List is tiered so Spencer can allocate 20 copies deliberately (keep a few in reserve)

## Constraints
- Handwritten notes are Spencer's — the dossier supplies hooks, not scripts (unless he asks)
- Outward contact (mailing, emailing) is Spencer's action; nothing is sent by the system
- US English throughout
