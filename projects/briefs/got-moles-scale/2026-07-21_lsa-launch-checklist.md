## What Google requires for pest control (US)

1. **Business details** — legal name, address, service areas, hours (must match GBP)
2. **License** — current state pest control applicator/operator license, verified annually
3. **Insurance** — general liability certificate (COI) upload
4. **Background checks** — business entity + owner; field techs who enter homes/yards
   (run by Google's screening partner, free, consent-based)
5. **GBP link** — LSA reviews pull from the Google Business Profile
