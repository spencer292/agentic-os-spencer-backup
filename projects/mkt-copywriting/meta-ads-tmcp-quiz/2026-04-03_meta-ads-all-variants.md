# A/B TEST RECOMMENDATIONS

1. **Campaign 1:** Test Ad 3 (specialist) vs Ad 1 (problem) as the first head-to-head. These hit different awareness levels — Ad 3 works for people already considering pest control, Ad 1 catches people still in the frustration stage.

2. **Campaign 2:** Test Ad 6 (cost) vs Ad 5 (prevention). Both strong, but the investment-protection angle (Ad 6) may resonate harder in Sammamish/Bellevue where home values and lawn spend are highest.

3. **Video vs static:** Run one static winner and one video in each campaign simultaneously. Meta's algorithm will auto-allocate spend to whichever performs — you'll know within the first week whether video justifies the production effort.

4. **Headline testing:** Within each winning ad, rotate the 5 headline variants. Headlines are the single biggest lever on CTR — let Meta's dynamic creative optimization handle this.
