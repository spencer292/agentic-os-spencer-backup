**Where it loses (the gaps):**
- **Two head informational terms — absent:** "how to get rid of moles in your yard" and "voles vs moles" are owned by **.edu extension services + national brands** (Scotts, Clemson/Penn State/MSU extensions, Orkin). These are exactly what AI Overviews summarize → biggest GEO opportunity, gated by external authority depth.
- **Two derm-leak queries:** "mole removal near me washington" and "how much does mole removal cost washington" return **dermatology/cosmetic** results, not pest. Highest risk for AI Overviews answering with a skin-mole answer.
- **Commercial pages don't rank:** homepage ~pos 19; city pages 20–60; **no money page in top 60** (GSC 2026-05-24).

**Competitors:** molemasters.biz (since 1991, 40+ city pages, pricing page — but **no visible review count or schema**) and nwmole.com recur most. Got Moles' review volume (219+) + schema + chemical-free niche are genuine edges competitors don't surface.
