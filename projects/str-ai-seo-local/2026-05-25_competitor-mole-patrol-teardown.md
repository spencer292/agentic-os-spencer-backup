# Competitive Teardown — Mole Patrol (and the real landscape) · 2026-05-25

**Premise check:** Roy asked us to investigate Mole Patrol "which seems to be beating Got Moles."
**Finding: in organic search, it isn't.** Got Moles out-ranks Mole Patrol on essentially every query
that matters. The investigation is still highly valuable — it corrected the premise and surfaced *who
actually* beats Got Moles, and where the real gaps are.

**Method:** live crawl of mole-patrol.com (structure/schema/content) + SERP head-to-head + reviews/age
research. **Not covered (tool limits):** the Google **map/local pack** (not visible in organic results),
true backlink data, and AI-panel citation. See the map-pack caveat in §6 — it's the one place Roy's
observation could still be true.

---

## 1. Headline

- **Mole Patrol is an *aged but weak* site.** Operating **since 1983 (43 yrs)** — but ~15 thin pages,
  **zero schema**, no city pages, no real blog, a broken meta description, per-mole pricing, and a
  **3.4★ Yelp with unreturned-call complaints**. Got Moles beats it on content, schema, AEO, reviews,
  and nearly all rankings.
- **Mole Patrol out-ranks Got Moles on exactly ONE query:** "mole control near me washington" (#4 vs GM
  absent). That's it.
- **The real organic threat is Mole Masters** (molemasters.biz) — #1 on *both* "mole exterminator
  seattle" and "best mole control company washington." If anyone is "beating" Got Moles on head terms,
  it's Mole Masters, not Mole Patrol.

## 4. Where Got Moles already wins (don't over-correct)
Content depth, schema/AEO, informational + city coverage, **review volume + consistency (219+ vs 3.4★ Yelp)**,
named veteran owner, transparency, and almost all city rankings. Mole Patrol is not the problem.

## 5. The real gaps + the real rival
- **Everett — Got Moles is absent** (Mole Patrol's home turf; pestmole.com / NW Mole Pros own it). Everett
  is a priority city in the internal-linking architecture — strengthen `/mole-control-everett/`.
- **"mole control near me washington" — Got Moles absent.** This is a GBP/local-pack + near-me play.
- **Mole Masters (molemasters.biz) is the actual head-term #1.** ~40+ city pages, since 1991, no visible
  reviews/schema. **This is the competitor that warrants the next teardown**, not Mole Patrol.
- **Derm hijack** persists on "mole removal washington" (lawn-signal disambiguation, per target-keywords Rule 1-2).

## 6. The map-pack caveat (where Roy's observation could still hold)
This analysis is **organic only**. The Google **3-pack/map** is a different ranking system driven by
**proximity + review count/recency + GBP age + citations** — exactly where Mole Patrol's 43-year local
authority is strongest, especially for North/Eastside "near me" searches. **It's plausible Mole Patrol
beats Got Moles in the map pack on the Eastside even while losing organic.** Worth confirming what Roy
actually saw (organic result, map pack, or an ad) — it changes the fix (content/links vs GBP/reviews/proximity).

## 7. Action plan (ranked)
1. **Confirm the observation** — ask Roy: where did Mole Patrol appear to win (organic, map pack, ad, which query/location)? Reconcile vs this data.
2. **Close the Everett gap** — strengthen `/mole-control-everett/` + Snohomish concentration (priority-city work already scoped).
3. **Capture "near me"** — GBP optimization + a near-me-intent angle; this is the one term Mole Patrol actually wins.
4. **Teardown Mole Masters next** — the genuine head-term rival (#1 on both broad queries).
5. **Exploit Mole Patrol's reputation weakness** — lead Eastside/North messaging with responsiveness + 219+ five-star (vs their 3.4★ Yelp / unreturned calls).
6. **Flat-rate vs per-mole** — message the $450-flat advantage (beats $130+$95/mole at 3-4+ moles; per-mole has a bad incentive) + add a dedicated **pricing page** (they have one; GM doesn't).
7. **Off-page authority** (the through-line) — Mole Patrol's only real edge is **aged citation breadth** (BBB/Angi/Checkbook/Yelp/HomeAdvisor/Nextdoor over 43 yrs). Got Moles should widen its directory/citation footprint + earn links → `str-authority-strategy`. This is the same lever the 2026-05-24 audit flagged for the commercial pages.
